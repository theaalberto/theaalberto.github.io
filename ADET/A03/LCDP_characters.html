La Casa De Papel Characters - Season 1

The Professor
The mastermind who planned the Royal Mint heist. Calm, methodical, and brilliant.
Tokyo
Fiery and impulsive, Tokyo narrates the story and plays a central role in the heist.
Berlin
The Professor’s brother and team leader inside the Mint. Charismatic and authoritative.
Nairobi
In charge of printing money, Nairobi is passionate and keeps morale high.
Rio
A young hacker and Tokyo’s love interest, Rio handles all tech operations.
Denver
Son of Moscow, known for his unique laugh and relationship with hostage Monica.
Moscow
Denver's father and former miner, responsible for digging the escape tunnel.
Helsinki
One of the muscle men. Loyal and quiet, he's also part of the Serbian war veteran duo.
Oslo
Helsinki's cousin and fellow enforcer. Strong but less outspoken.
Monica (Stockholm)
Initially a hostage, Monica falls for Denver and later joins the crew as Stockholm.
Raquel Murillo
Police inspector leading the negotiations, who later falls for the Professor.
