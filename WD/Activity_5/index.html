JAPAN

Japan is an island nation in East Asia, located in the Pacific Ocean. It consists of four main
islands—Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu, and Shikoku—as well as numerous smaller ones.
Known for its blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge modernity, Japan has a rich
cultural heritage that includes samurai, geisha, tea ceremonies, and Shinto shrines,
alongside futuristic cities like Tokyo, renowned for its technology and innovation.
